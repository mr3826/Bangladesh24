# Content Rules

1. Only publish stories with a clear source link.
2. Prefer official or primary reporting for weather, public safety, prices, and government notices.
3. Do not make claims that are stronger than the source article.
4. Use simple Bangla and avoid sensational wording.
5. Keep scripts under 60 seconds for Reels-first output.
6. Do not auto-post in the MVP. Export and manually review first.
